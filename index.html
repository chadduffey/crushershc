About Us

We're a family orientated hockey club based on the Central Coast of NSW who compete in the Coast Hockey Association (CCHA) competition. Our games are played on the turf fields in Wyong on Friday nights & Saturday afternoons.

In 2025 we'll field teams in both Div 1 mens and Div 3 mens divisions.

We're always on the lookout for new players so please get in touch if you are keen for a run!
